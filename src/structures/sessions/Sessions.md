# 🌟 Session High-Low Identification Algorithm

This algorithm detects the **highs** and **lows** during the market sessions: **Asia**, **Europe**, and **America**, helping traders assess market trends.

---

## 🧠 Core Principles

### 1️⃣ **Market Sessions**
- **Sydney Session**: 21:00 UTC - :00 UTC
- **Europe Session**: 08:00 UTC - 16:00 UTC
- **America Session**: 16:00 UTC - 00:00 UTC

### 2️⃣ **Session High and Low**
- During each session, the algorithm tracks:
  - **Session High**: The highest price during the session.
  - **Session Low**: The lowest price during the session.
- These are updated continuously throughout the session.

---

## ⚙️ Dynamic Updates
- The high and low are updated in real-time as price moves within each session.

---

## ⚙️ Summary
- **Track session highs and lows**: Real-time updates for each market session (Asia, Europe, America).

---

## 🚀 Features
- **Real-time tracking** of session highs and lows.
- **Clear session boundaries** for precise market analysis.
